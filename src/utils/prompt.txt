Lord of the mysteries: There are 7 Orthodox gods within this world: The eternal blazing sun, the lord of the storms, the earth mother, the evernight goddess, the god of steam and machinery, the god of wisdom and knowledge and the god of combat. Within this world, there are people known as beyonders who control supernatural powers. Their abilities are ranked based on Sequences, range from 0 to 9, with 22 pathways: Seer, Marauder, Apprentice, Spectator, Bard, Sailor, Reader, Secrets Suppliant, Sleepless, Corpse Collector, Warrior, Assassin, Hunter, Mystery Pryer, Savant, Monster, Planter, Apothecary, Criminal, Prisoner, Lawyer, and Arbiter. Each pathway corresponds to one of the major arcana cards. For instance, the Seer pathway corresponds to The Fool, with Spectator corresponding to Justice and so on. In order to become a beyonder one has to consume what's known as a "Beyonder Potion" that corresponds to one of the 22 pathways. You start of as a Sequence 9. Once consumed, you are unable to switch to a different pathway, unless you've become a sequence 5, in which case you may switch to one of the neighboring pathways. Seer, Marauder and Apprentice for instance are neighboring pathways, with Spectator, Bard, Sailor, Reader and Secrets Suppliant corresponding to another group. Sequence 5 and onwards, a ritual is required for the consumption of potions. Loss of control will occur if the ritual is incomplete. Once you become a Sequence 4, you become a demigod and  you obtain an incomplete mythical creature form. Sequence 3 has no changes with the exclusion of pathway specific features, however beyonders at that sequence are referred to as Saints. At Sequence 2, one completes their mythical creature form and are referred to as Angels. Sequence 1 beyonders can attempt to do a ritual where two other sequence 1 beyonder characteristics and the uniqueness corresponding  to the pathway is required in order to advance to Sequence 0, where one becomes a true deity and holds authority over their pathway and it's symbolism. A Great Old One, also known as Above The Sequence, is an existence who holds authority over all neighboring pathway's uniquenesses and their sefirot. For instance, the existence known as the Great Old One for Spectator, Bard, Sailor, Reader and Secrets Suppliant is known as "God Almighty", holding the respective authorities of Imagination, Light, Calamities, Omniscience and Degeneration. Angel families are families in which the ancestor was an angel. Such families include the Zaratul family and Antigonus of the Seer pathway, Abraham family of the Apprentice pathway, Zoroast and Amon family of the Marauder pathway, Medici family and Einhorn family of the Hunter pathway, Augustus family of the arbiter and lawyer pathways. Some churchs have their own organizations of beyonders. For instance, The Church of evernight has "Nighthawks", the church of steam and machinery has "Machinery hivemind", the church of the storms has "Mandated Punishers". Members of these organizations are known as Official Beyonders, and work with law enforcement. There are also secret organizations that arent managed by the churches. For instance, the demonness sect, aurora order and the secret order. There are 22 major arcana cards made by the former emperor of the Intis empire called "Blasphemy Cards", holding traits and abilities of the pathway they correspond to. These cards have anti-divination and anti-tracking properties, making them extremely valuable and powerful.
